Questions

Good to know

Is this financial advice or a signal service?

No. The Wheel Room is educational. The tool, the seasons reads, and the "at your rules" ideas are a framework and a starting point for your own decisions — not instructions to buy or sell. You are always responsible for your own trades.

Do I have to connect my brokerage account?

No. You log your trades (or paste them from your broker's export). Nothing here asks for your brokerage password, and any hosted version would only ever use official, read-only broker connections — never your credentials.

I'm new to the wheel. Is this too advanced?

You'll get more out of it if you know the basics of cash-secured puts and covered calls, but the seasons framework and the Coach are built to teach as you go. Join the waitlist and grow into it.

What does it cost?

Founding-member pricing will be shared when doors open — and waitlist members lock the lowest rate for life. Joining the list costs nothing and isn't a commitment.

Will my data be private?

Yes. Your logged positions are yours. The seasons reads and community are shared; your account and P&L are not.
